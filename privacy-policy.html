Softlight — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

No data is sent to us

Softlight is a browser extension that helps reduce eye strain by dimming bright web pages. The extension operates entirely on your device. We do not operate servers that collect user information, and we do not receive, store, process, or sell any information about your browsing activity or use of the extension.

Information Stored by the Extension

Softlight stores only the preferences necessary for the extension to function, including:

  • Whether dimming is enabled
  • Dimming intensity level
  • Tint color preferences
  • Sites where Softlight has been disabled

These preferences are stored using Chrome's storage.sync feature. If you are signed into Chrome, Google may synchronize these settings across your own devices as part of your browser profile. This synchronization is handled by Google and is not accessible to us.

Information We Do Not Collect

Softlight does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track browsing activity
  • Collect browsing history
  • Use analytics or telemetry
  • Serve advertisements
  • Sell, share, or transfer data to third parties
  • Transmit page content or browsing information anywhere

The extension only applies a visual dimming effect to pages that you view. It does not inspect, record, or transmit the content of those pages.

Permissions

Softlight requests access to websites so that the dimming effect can be applied to pages you visit. It also requests storage permission to save your preferences between browsing sessions. These permissions are used solely to provide the extension's functionality and are not used for data collection.

Third-Party Services

Softlight does not use third-party analytics services, advertising networks, tracking tools, or external APIs.
